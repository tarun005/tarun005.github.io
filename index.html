Tarun Kalluri

I am a first-year PhD student in the CSE department at UCSD. My research interests span the areas of computer vision and machine learning. I am particularly interested in learning compact embeddings of images and videos from limited labeled data that are useful across a wide range of domains and distributions. Our group at UCSD works on a wider variety of problems in computer vision involving graphics, 3D reconstruction, material estimation and robotic vision.

Prior to this, I was a research assistant at Centre for Visual Information and Technology (CVIT), IIIT Hyderabad advised by Prof. CV Jawahar . I worked on various problems related to semantic understanding from large scale text and image data using techniques in deep learning.

Even before that, I spent a year working as a data scientist at Oracle India, Bangalore. I completed my undergraduation from IIT Guwahati in 2016, with a major in ECE and a minor in CSE. I did my bachelor's thesis on Stochastic Wireless Networks, a field that has kept me busy for long before I started pursuing deep learning.
