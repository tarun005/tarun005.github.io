Tarun Kalluri

I am a first-year PhD student at the Centre for Visual Computing at UCSD advised by Prof. Manmohan Chandrakar. My research interests span the areas of computer vision and machine learning. I work on problems related to unsupervised learning, domain adaptation and transferable representation learning for images. Our group at UCSD works on a wider variety of problems in computer vision involving graphics, 3D reconstruction, material estimation and robotic vision. You can find my CV here.
